Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 Aug 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. This summary is for clarity and is not legal advice.

1. Who we are

“icando”, the introvert‑friendly social skills app. Contact: privacy@icando.app.

2. Data we collect

  • Account Data: email address (if you create an account) and hashed identifiers.
  • App Content: lesson progress, quest completions, preferences (e.g., reminders).
  • Conversation Data: messages you exchange with the AI during practice, necessary to generate responses and provide feedback.
  • Device & Usage: app version, iOS version, device model, crash logs, and basic analytics (screens viewed, feature usage).
  • Payments: handled by Apple. We receive purchase status (e.g., active subscription) but not full card details.
  • Cookies/Local Storage: used to remember preferences on the website; minimal and not for advertising.

3. How we use data

  • Provide and improve app features (lessons, quests, AI feedback).
  • Operate, troubleshoot, and secure our services.
  • Personalize recommendations and track learning progress.
  • Communicate about updates, tips, and support (you can opt out of non‑essential emails).
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse.

4. AI processing & retention

Conversation Data may be processed by our AI providers to generate replies and quality feedback. We retain practice transcripts for a limited period to provide features like review history and recommendations, then either delete or de‑identify them. You can request deletion sooner (see Your Choices).

5. Legal bases (EEA/UK)

We process data on the basis of: (a) contract (to provide the app), (b) legitimate interests (to improve and secure services), and (c) consent where required (e.g., optional communications).

6. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only with service providers who help us run the app (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, cloud hosting, AI processing) under strict confidentiality and security commitments. Purchases are processed by Apple.

7. Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, after which we delete or de‑identify it. Retention periods vary by data type and legal requirements.

8. Your choices & rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Export a copy of your data.
  • Opt out of non‑essential emails.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise rights, email privacy@icando.app. We may ask to verify your identity.

9. Security

We use industry‑standard safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, monitoring). No method is 100% secure; please avoid sharing sensitive personal data in practice chats.

10. International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. When we transfer data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

11. Children

icando is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children under that age.

12. Third‑party links

Our website or app may link to third‑party sites. Their privacy practices are their own.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will change the “Effective date” above and, when appropriate, notify you in‑app or by email.

14. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@icando.app or support@icando.app.
