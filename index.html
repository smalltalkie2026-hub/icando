Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Make sure you’re on the latest version of icando. Check the App Store for updates.
  2. Force‑quit and reopen the app. This clears temporary state.
  3. If a quest or chat is stuck, switch networks (Wi‑Fi ↔︎ cellular) and try again.
  4. Restart your device. Many intermittent issues resolve after a reboot.
  5. If problems continue, email support@icando.app with your device model, iOS version, icando version, and screenshots.

Known issues & status: see status notes.

Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is icando?

icando is an introvert‑friendly app to learn and practice social skills using lessons, quick reviews, real‑world quests, and AI chat role‑plays.

Is icando free?

You can start for free. A paid plan unlocks additional lessons, advanced quests, and deeper AI feedback.

Do I need an account?

You can browse free content without an account. Creating an account syncs progress and enables personalized recommendations.

How does AI chat practice work?

Pick a scenario (e.g., “meeting someone new”). The bot role‑plays with you and offers instant feedback on tone, clarity, and empathy.

What data does icando collect?

We collect minimal data to operate the app, such as account email and usage analytics. See the privacy section for details and choices.

How do I request a refund?

For App Store subscriptions, refunds are handled by Apple. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com and follow the instructions.

Accessibility support

icando supports Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, and prefers‑reduced‑motion. Email us if you encounter issues.

Service status & known issues

All systems operational. No major incidents reported. Minor: rare timeouts on poor networks during AI chat; retry usually succeeds.
